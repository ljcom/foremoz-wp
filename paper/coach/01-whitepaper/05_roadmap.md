# Foremoz Coach Whitepaper v0.2 - Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Coach Micro-site Foundation

- `coach.foremoz.com/<coach_handle>` public page.
- profile + offer publishing.
- basic subscribe CTA.
- free tier launch (minimum fitur wajib tersedia).

## Phase 2 - Social Share and Attribution

- one-click copy/share links ke WhatsApp/Instagram/TikTok.
- campaign params tracking.
- funnel dashboard (share/click/subscribe).

## Phase 3 - Join Class by Location

- class catalog by location.
- slot availability + booking flow.
- booking confirmation dan attendance trace.

## Phase 4 - Invitation Network Expansion

- `coach invite member`.
- `coach invite studio`.
- `studio invite coach`.
- `member invite friend`.

## Phase 5 - Support Team Service Tier

- role management untuk tim coach.
- onsite re-registration workflow.
- assisted check-in and verification operations.

## Phase 6 - Performance and Governance

- coach KPI dashboard.
- retention/conversion baseline.
- governance hooks untuk tenant/gov policy.

## Phase 7 - Pricing Maturity

- tier packaging refinement: `free`, `starter`, `growth`, `pro/team`.
- billing + upgrade self-serve dari workspace coach.
- pricing experiment framework dengan guardrail conversion dan churn.
