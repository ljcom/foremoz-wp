# 5. Conclusion and Future Work

## 5.1 Conclusion
This paper presents a triadic interaction model with activity-verified identity, enabling structured, scalable, and multi-domain gig economy participation. The model addresses limitations of existing platforms and introduces a new paradigm based on interaction and verified identity.

## 5.2 Future Work
Future research should focus on prototype implementation, empirical validation, integration with verification technologies, and expansion across industries. These directions are necessary to test the operational feasibility of the model and to assess its broader applicability in real-world gig economy contexts.
