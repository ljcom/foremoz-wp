# Foremoz Gov Whitepaper v0.1 - Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Governance Foundation

- restricted gov dashboard shell.
- privileged auth + role guard.
- immutable governance action logging.

## Phase 2 - Account Intervention Controls

- tenant suspend/unsuspend controls.
- account disable/enable controls.
- reason and approval metadata enforcement.

## Phase 3 - Pricing and Policy Controls

- global baseline pricing.
- tenant price override.
- promotion/policy control matrix.

## Phase 4 - Monitoring and Risk Intelligence

- activity telemetry aggregation.
- anomaly detection feed.
- alerting and resolution workflow.

## Phase 5 - Income and Platform Reporting

- income dashboard lintas tenant.
- MRR/revenue trend views.
- governance impact analysis pada income metrics.

## Phase 6 - Hardening and Compliance

- approval workflow hardening.
- policy rollback playbook.
- audit export and forensic support.
