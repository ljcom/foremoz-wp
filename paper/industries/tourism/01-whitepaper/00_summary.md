# Foremoz Tourism Whitepaper - Summary

Tourism diposisikan sebagai **Phase 2** dengan pendekatan bertahap dimulai dari Tourism Lite.

## Why Phase 2

Tourism memiliki kompleksitas operasional tinggi:
- multi-day itinerary
- group logistics
- transport and accommodation dependencies
- multi-party coordination

Karena itu, Tourism diluncurkan setelah core collaboration dan transaction layer stabil.

## Tourism Lite Entry

Entry paling aman:
- city walking tour
- food tour
- museum/heritage tour
- bike/photography tour

Model ini tetap event-centric dan kompatibel dengan core platform.

## Domain Focus

- guide-led experiences
- participant group journeys
- place-based event operations
- itinerary progression

## Economic Model in Tourism

- per-tour ticket/booking
- package-based monetization bertahap
- sponsor and partner activation
- potential affiliate/revenue-share model

## Strategic Position

Tourism adalah ekspansi natural Foremoz ke experience travel domain, dimulai dari struktur ringan sebelum full package orchestration.
