# Foremoz Performance Whitepaper (Industry Draft)

Dokumen ini adalah whitepaper industry-level untuk vertikal **performance**.

Performance mencakup:
- music performance
- theater
- stand-up comedy
- dance
- magic show
- street performance
- festival
- cultural performance

## Scope
- Live performance events
- Talent collaboration, stage slots, run-of-show
- Audience engagement and attendance

## Relation to General WP
- Architecture, actor model, dan identity mengikuti dokumen general:
  - `paper/general/01-whitepaper/foremoz-event-os-whitepaper-v1.md`
- Sponsorship untuk saat ini mengikuti framework di whitepaper general.

## 1. Performer Layer

Performer adalah pusat vertical ini.

Contoh performer:
- musician
- band
- DJ
- comedian
- actor
- dancer
- magician
- street artist

Fitur performer profile:
- bio
- genre/style
- previous shows
- followers
- media portfolio

Contoh:
- Band: Midnight Echo
- Genre: Indie Rock
- Followers: 3,200
- Shows: 45

## 2. Show / Performance Event

Event pada vertical ini biasanya disebut show.

Contoh:
- Live Music Night
- Stand-up Comedy Show
- Dance Performance
- Theater Play

Fitur:
- show schedule
- lineup
- duration
- capacity
- ticket tiers

## 3. Lineup Management

Performance sering memiliki lineup:
- opening act
- main act
- closing act

Fitur:
- lineup order
- stage time
- artist collaboration

Contoh:
- 20:00 Opening band
- 21:00 Main act
- 22:30 DJ set

## 4. Ticketing System

Performance sangat bergantung pada ticketing.

Fitur:
- ticket types
- VIP ticket
- early bird
- group ticket

Contoh:
- Early Bird: $15
- Regular: $25
- VIP: $50

## 5. Venue Layer

Venue sangat penting.

Contoh venue:
- music club
- concert hall
- bar
- theater
- festival ground
- street stage

Venue profile bisa memiliki:
- capacity
- stage equipment
- sound system
- lighting

## 6. Production Layer

Performance sering membutuhkan production team.

Role tambahan:
- stage manager
- sound engineer
- lighting engineer
- crew

Platform dapat mendukung:
- crew invitation
- production checklist
- technical rider

## 7. Media Layer

Performance cocok dengan media distribution.

Fitur:
- show recording
- live streaming
- highlight clips
- photo gallery

Contoh:
- Watch live show replay

## 8. Fan / Audience Layer

Audience bukan hanya pembeli tiket, tetapi fans.

Fitur:
- follow artist
- fan club
- show history
- favorite artist

Contoh:
- Samuel follows Midnight Echo

## 9. Merchandising

Performance kuat di merchandise.

Contoh:
- band t-shirt
- album
- poster
- limited edition merch

Platform dapat mendukung:
- pre-order merch
- event merch store

## 10. Sponsorship

Brand sering sponsor performance.

Contoh sponsor:
- beer brand
- energy drink
- fashion brand
- music gear brand

Bentuk sponsor:
- title sponsor
- stage sponsor
- brand booth

## 11. Fan Engagement

Fans sering ingin berinteraksi.

Fitur:
- meet & greet
- fan voting
- request song
- fan message

Contoh:
- Vote next song

## 12. Artist Passport

Passport dalam performance bisa menyimpan:
- shows performed
- venues performed
- audience size
- collaborations

Contoh:
- Shows: 120
- Cities: 8
- Venues: 25

## 13. Audience Passport

Audience juga memiliki history:
- shows attended
- favorite artist
- festival history

Contoh:
- Attended 15 concerts

Ini menciptakan performance identity pada audience.

## 14. Festival Layer

Performance sering berkembang menjadi festival.

Contoh:
- Jazz Festival
- Comedy Festival
- Dance Festival

Festival biasanya memiliki:
- multi-day
- multi-stage
- many artists

## 15. Discovery Layer

Audience bisa menemukan show melalui:
- artist yang di-follow
- genre
- location
- friends attending

Contoh:
- Shows near you

## 16. Monetization

Vertical performance memiliki monetisasi kuat:
- ticket revenue (concert ticket, festival pass)
- merchandise (shirts, albums, posters)
- sponsorship (brand activation, stage sponsor)
- streaming (paid live stream)

## 17. Why Performance Vertical Works

Performance memiliki:
- strong fan identity
- repeat attendance
- media value
- merchandise economy

Ini cocok untuk event-based platform.

## 18. Struktur Event OS

Dalam Foremoz:
- Performer -> Show
- Audience -> Attend
- Venue -> Host
- Sponsor -> Support

Semua tetap berpusat pada event/show.

## Kesimpulan

Performance vertical dapat mencakup fungsi:
- artist profile
- show creation
- lineup management
- ticketing
- fan following
- media distribution
- merchandise
- festival management
- sponsorship

Semua tetap kompatibel dengan arsitektur Event OS yang sama dengan fitness dan seminar.
