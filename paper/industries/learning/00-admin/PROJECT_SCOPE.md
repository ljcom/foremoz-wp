# PROJECT_SCOPE.md
Foremoz Learning – Scope Definition

## Objective
Dokumen ini menetapkan batas cakupan untuk whitepaper dan implementasi vertikal Learning.

## In Scope
- Program dan session untuk seminar, workshop, course, bootcamp, mentoring, dan training
- Actor learning: instructor, participant, organizer, venue, sponsor
- Enrollment, attendance, progress, material, certificate
- Integrasi dengan Foremoz Passport (learning identity)

## Out of Scope
- Domain non-learning yang tidak terkait learning event lifecycle
- Kustomisasi enterprise per-client di luar blueprint produk
- Perubahan arsitektur platform-level tanpa decision log

## Relation to General Whitepaper
Semua keputusan platform-level merujuk ke:
- paper/general/01-whitepaper/foremoz-event-os-whitepaper-v1.md
