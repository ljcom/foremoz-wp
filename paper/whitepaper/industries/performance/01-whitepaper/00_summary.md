# Foremoz Performance Whitepaper - Summary

Performance mencakup creator-led entertainment events seperti music, theater, comedy, dance, dan festival.

Modul ini menggunakan core yang sama, lalu menambah kebutuhan khas industri pertunjukan: lineup, production, fan engagement, dan merch.

## Domain Focus

- show creation and run-of-show
- lineup and stage-time orchestration
- production team coordination
- fan layer and media distribution
- merchandise and sponsorship activation

## Industry-Specific Capabilities

- ticketing tiers: early bird, regular, VIP, group
- lineup management: opening/main/closing acts
- production roles: stage manager, sound, lighting, crew
- fan engagement: follow artist, meet and greet, fan voting
- media layer: recording, highlight, replay distribution

## Economic Model in Performance

- ticket revenue
- merch and add-on sales
- sponsor-backed show activation
- creator-host commercial collaboration per event

## Strategic Position

Performance memperkuat creator economy angle Foremoz dengan frekuensi event tinggi, identitas fan yang kuat, dan peluang monetisasi media yang luas.
