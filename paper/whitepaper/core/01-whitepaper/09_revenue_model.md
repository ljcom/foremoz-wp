# 9. Why Foremoz Is Different

Foremoz berbeda dari platform event biasa karena menggabungkan secara terpadu:
- Passport identity layer
- Creator/Participant/Host role system
- event-centric economic model
- collaboration infrastructure
- sponsorship infrastructure
- cross-vertical extensibility

Karena itu Foremoz melampaui:
- simple ticketing systems
- generic event directories
- venue booking-only systems
- isolated single-industry apps

## Foremoz Platform Thesis

Foremoz exists to empower experience creators to build communities, run events, and carry reputation across industries through Passport and Event OS infrastructure.
